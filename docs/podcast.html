Podcast on Unified Field Theory (UFT)

The source is a GitHub repository containing a proposed Unified Field Theory (UFT) that attempts to unify fundamental forces and particles in physics. The theory reinterprets concepts like inertia, gravity, and dark matter as manifestations of wave functions emitted by mass, suggesting a more unified view of the universe.

The repository presents hypothetical frameworks, mathematical models, and research notes supporting the theory, along with a discussion of how UFT explains various phenomena like atomic cohesion, electrostatic and magnetic forces, and quantum entanglement.

The project encourages collaborative contributions and offers further exploration into the hierarchical structure of the universe using graph theory.
